# Research notes

The documents in this directory are **non-normative** and **point-in-time**. Each one backs a specific claim made in the [specification](../spec.md) or the [primer](../primer.md) with a prior-art survey or due-diligence pass, and each states its own method and verification status.

These notes age faster than the specification does. The date in each filename marks when the survey was done; treat conclusions as snapshots, not standing facts.

Corrections are contributions. If a document here misdescribes a system you know well, an issue pointing at the primary source is the most useful thing you can send.
